ABOUT Me

Welcome to the inspiring journey of Shanti Timalsina Sharma, where empowerment meets creativity and beauty.

We Empower Life

Shanti Timalsina Sharma is an accomplished entrepreneur, renowned beautician, motivational speaker, and dedicated social worker. With a mission to uplift and empower individuals, she combines her expertise in beauty with her passion for personal development. Shanti believes that true beauty radiates from within, and her work reflects this philosophy as she helps others discover their potential. Through various initiatives, she advocates for women's empowerment and actively supports underprivileged communities, inspiring countless lives along the way.

Nepali Congress

Nepali Congress is one of the oldest and most prominent political parties in Nepal. Founded in 1950, it has played a significant role in shaping the political landscape of the country. The party is committed to democracy, social justice, and economic development, aiming to empower the citizens of Nepal through progressive policies.

Nepali Congress has been at the forefront of major movements in Nepal's history, advocating for human rights, inclusive governance, and sustainable development. Its vision includes fostering unity among diverse communities and driving national progress through education, health, and infrastructure development.

Federation of Nepali Cottage and Small Industries (FNCSI)

The Federation of Nepali Cottage and Small Industries (FNCSI) is dedicated to supporting and promoting the growth of small and cottage industries across Nepal. With a mission to empower local entrepreneurs, FNCSI provides essential resources, training, and advocacy to enhance productivity and sustainability within this vital sector.

FNCSI plays a crucial role in fostering an environment where small businesses can thrive. Through various initiatives, we aim to equip entrepreneurs with the skills and knowledge necessary to navigate the market, improve their operations, and contribute significantly to Nepal's economic development.

Mitini Nepal

Mitini Nepal is an inspiring organization dedicated to empowering women and promoting gender equality in Nepal. Founded with the vision of creating a society where women have equal opportunities in all aspects of life, Mitini Nepal focuses on enhancing women's leadership and participation in various sectors, including education, health, and entrepreneurship.

Through workshops, training programs, and community outreach initiatives, Mitini Nepal aims to equip women with the skills and confidence they need to succeed. By fostering a supportive network and providing resources for personal and professional growth, Mitini Nepal is transforming lives and creating a brighter future for women across the country.
